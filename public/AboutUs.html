Care & Share is a volunteer organisation which is dedicated to fostering a community-driven approach to healthcare, where compassion and support intersect to create lasting change. Our volunteer organization is founded on the belief that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, regardless of their circumstances.

Care & Share operates at the intersection of empathy and expertise. Our volunteers, comprised of healthcare professionals, advocates, and passionate individuals, work tirelessly to address healthcare disparities and promote wellness within our communities. Through our diverse range of initiatives, we strive to make a meaningful impact on the lives of individuals and families facing health challenges. From providing essential medical services to underprivileged populations to advocating for healthcare policy reform, we are driven by a shared vision of a healthier, more equitable future for all.

People helping people. You can be one of the helpers too.

Together we achieve more

At Care & Share, collaboration is at the heart of everything we do. We partner with local healthcare providers, community organizations, and stakeholders to amplify our efforts and maximize our reach. Together, we cultivate a culture of care, compassion, and empowerment, where every member of our community can thrive.
